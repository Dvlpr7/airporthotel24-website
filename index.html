About us - Airport hotel 24

Airport Hotel 24 is a hotel located in Tashkent. Other amenities include a restaurant, shared lounge and food delivery service, as well as free Wi-Fi. A 24-hour front desk, concierge and currency exchange services are available. The rooms have a desk and kettle, as well as a shower and free toiletries. Other amenities include air conditioning and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Airport Hotel 24 provides bed linen and towels. Guests can enjoy a buffet, continental or halal breakfast. Tashkent International Airport named after Islam Karimov is 2 km away.
